Lime Cake

This lime cake is a tender, buttery dessert that balances sweet vanilla undertones with the sharp, vibrant tang of fresh citrus. It features a tight but incredibly moist crumb, thanks to the combination of creamed butter, vegetable oil, and tangy sour cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package lemon cake mix
  • 1 ⅓ cups vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup orange juice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 (3 ounce) package lime flavored Jell-O mix
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease three 8-inch round cake pans.
  2. To make the cake: Beat cake mix, oil, orange juice, eggs, and gelatin mix in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Pour into the prepared cake pans.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cake layer comes out clean, 16 to 18 minutes. Allow cake layers to cool completely.
  5. Meanwhile, make frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  6. Add confectioners' sugar and lime juice; mix until well combined.
  7. Fill and frost cooled cake layers.
